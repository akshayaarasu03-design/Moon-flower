The Moon Flower is a symbol of blossoming in dark times.
It represents us in every way possible.
We loved when we weren’t supposed to,
when we were not meant to be—yet somehow, we found each other.
We craved each other.
We chose each other.
I see how hard you try.
I see your patience, your effort,
your silence when things are tough.
This flower is for the parts of you most people don’t notice—
but I do, Dinesh.
